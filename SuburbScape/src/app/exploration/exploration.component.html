"What housing opportunities and features does regional Victoria provide?"

** Hover for more details

Diverse Architecture - Houses in regional Victoria can exhibit a wide range of architectural styles, reflecting the historical & cultural influences.
Affordability - Houses are more affordable than those in major cities like Melbourne.
Lifestyle and Scenery - Regional Victoria offers a picturesque & diverse landscape, with coastal towns, agricultural regions, wine-producing areas.
Community Atmosphere - Smaller populations can lead to closer connections among neighbors and a slower-paced, more communal way of life.
Commuting - Commuting times can be shorter in regional areas compared to big cities.
